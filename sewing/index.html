Erin Mackey

BFA Drama, BSc Computer Science, IATSE 212 Permittee

Calgary, AB

I specialize in clothing alterations, custom-built costumes, custom pattern drafting, and pattern digitization. Whether you need a simple hem or a fully custom costume, I'm here to bring your vision to life.

Services

Clothing Alterations

Hemming, resizing, repairs, and more...

Custom-Built Costumes

Tailored to your specifications for any event or production.

Custom Pattern Drafting

Unique patterns created to your measurements.

Pattern Digitization

Converting physical patterns into digital formats.
