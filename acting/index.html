Erin Mackey

BFA Drama, ACTRA Apprentice

Calgary, AB

Erin is a versatile artist with a passion for theatre and the performing arts. She is involved in various aspects of theatre production, including acting, costume-making, and technical work. Erin's love for performing extends outside the world of theatre, in her free time you can find Erin dancing and practicing gymnastics. She is an artist who continuously seeks to improve her skills and collaborate with others to create compelling performances.
