Erin Mackey

Calgary, AB

I specialize in creating unforgettable experiences through beginner ballroom dance lessons for weddings, expert dress and suit alterations, and custom dresses tailored to your unique style. When you're preparing for your big day I'm here to help you shine.

Services

Dance Lessons

Planning a wedding and want to dazzle your guests with a beautiful first dance? I offer personalized beginner ballroom dance lessons that are perfect for couples looking to make their wedding day extra special. Let's create a dance that's uniquely yours!

Alterations

Need a perfect fit? I provide professional dress and suit alterations to ensure you look your best for any occasion. From simple hemming to intricate adjustments, I take care of every detail.

Custom Dresses

Dreaming of a custom dress that reflects your personality and style? I offer custom dress design services, from concept to creation. Whether you're looking for a bridal gown, evening dress, or something special for any occasion, I'll work with you to bring your vision to life.
