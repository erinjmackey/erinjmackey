Erin Mackey

BFA Drama, BSc Computer Science, IATSE 212 Permittee

Calgary, AB

A recent graduate with a BFA in Drama from the University of Calgary, Erin Mackey is a new professional in costume and prop design for theatre and film. Known for meticulous craftsmanship and a unique aesthetic, Erin has contributed to a diverse array of productions, from period costumes to creative props. Beyond the studio, a passion for gymnastics, dance, acting, singing, and video games infuses dynamic energy into every project.
